A Kid and a Stick

Short Story Chapter

While walking, a kid finds a stick lying in the road.

"Oh, . . A stick.”

Intrigued, he picks it up.

Hmmm. . .

Holding it, he looks around the area, and finds a small plant on the sidewalk. *swings* He then swings the stick hard, destroying the plant in one strike.

Nice, looking good.

Feeling satisfied he then decided to do something.

Stick's ATK: 1 Plant’s HP: 1

He gave stats to both the stick and the plant as if it was something from a game.

This makes like I am playing inside an RPG.

He swings the stick once again, as if it was a sword from a game. He walks away with a smile on his face, as he holds the stick across his shoulder, feeling like he is holding a real sword.

“Let’s find some targets!”

As he said that, he sounds joyful, hopping while walking eager to find a new target. . . . A few minutes after walking he comes across a trash can full of soft drink cans. He picked up one of the cans, and place it in the ground. He takes a step back, holding his stick with both hands, and aims it at the soft drinks can on the ground. He then raises both of his hands and then. . . *swings* *clang* The can made a loud sound, and slightly move from its position.

“Wow!”

Fascinated at the fact that he couldn’t destroy the can with one swing, he only made a small dent in the can.

“One more time.”

*swings* *clang* He swings the stick one more time, but it was not enough to fully dent the can. Without thinking much, he swings the stick multiple times until he managed to fully dent the soft drink can.

“Finally. . .”

The soft drinks can is completely dented and lying on the floor. The kid learned now that it would take several hits before he can fully destroy a can of soft drinks, and so he concluded that:

Soft Drinks Can’s HP: 5

As he is required to swing five times before he can fully dent a can of soft drinks.

A lot harder than I thought, but I like it.

Excitement filled the kid as he felt like he was embarking on an adventure, seeking out targets to fight and defeat.

“Onto the next one!”

Having fun with his adventure he proceeds on finding another target he can hit.

Stick’s ATK: 1 - Plant’s HP: 1 Soft Drinks Can’s HP: 5 Cardboard Box’s HP: 12 Plastic Bottle’s HP: 2 Metal Lid’s HP: 2 Plastic Container’s HP: 3 Glass Bottle’s HP: 1 Tin Can’s HP: 3 Cobweb’s HP: 1 Banana’s HP: 1 - Stone’s HP: 9999999 Tree Branch’s HP: 9999999 Coconut’s HP: 9999999

Using a paper, he wrote the stats he gathered from hitting the things that are listed. There are some things he can hit and cause dent, and even destroy, but there are also things he can’t destroy. He then writes another thing:

Insects HP:

“Hahaha, how many hits do I need to do to kill an insect.”

With a smile on his face, he now plans on finding and killing an insect he could find. He then closes the notebook planning to go outside and find an insect he could hit. *buzz*

“What?”

Shocked by the sudden sound, he looks around him trying to find where the sound came from. After a few glances, he found something black attached to a wall, he looks closely, and he found something he could hit.

“This is perfect.”

He rushes outside to get his stick. It was hidden outside the house as he didn’t bring it inside the house to avoid being scolded. He knew that no one is at home, and this is a perfect situation. Now he can bring the stick inside in this situation. After getting the stick he rushes inside the living room.

“Here you are.”

He slowly walks towards the cockroach. Slowly raising both of his hands while holding the stick, he takes a stance and. . . *swings* *thud* The cockroach fell to the floor after getting hit by the stick, leaving what seems a liquid behind where the cockroach was hit.

“Is this the blood?”

The kid wonders, but he immediately looks at the floor where the cockroach fell. To his surprise, the cockroach is still moving. *swings* *thud*

“What?”

Surprised by the fact that the cockroach is still moving he swings again. *swings* *thud* With the cockroach slowly dying, the wings and its limbs are falling apart from each hit of the stick. After hitting it four times, the cockroach finally stops moving.

“Finally.”

The kid picked up the dead body of the cockroach with his bare hands, he then throws it outside the house.

“Bye.”

Cockroach’s HP: 4

He concluded that it takes four hits to kill a cockroach. After throwing the cockroach he goes back to the room. There are liquids from the wall and the floor, and there is also a bad scent coming from it.

“Ahhh, smells bad.”

Thinking he might get scolded by his parents, he grabs a towel in the kitchen and wipes the liquid on the wall and the floor. After wiping the liquid, he submerges the towel in the water in the kitchen sink.

“Oh, that was it?”

After hitting the grasshopper once, it immediately dies.

Grasshopper’s HP: 1

Looking at the dead grasshopper one final time, he stands up and walks away. While walking, his mind is full of anticipation in finding another thing he can hit with his stick. As he walks, he came across the lake in the area. He stopped walking and looks at it.

“Hmmm. . .”

After looking at it for a few seconds he decided to approach the lake. He then takes a stance, and. . . *swings* *splash*

“Woah that was harder than I thought.”

Surprised that the water is hard, he then swings again. *swings* *splash* After swinging the stick multiple times, he became drenched in water. However, he didn't stop, he continued hitting the water. While swinging the stick, suddenly. . .

“Ah---”

*splash* Someone pushed him to the lake. Now engulf in the lake’s water, he immediately stands up and turns around.

“It’s the weird kid with a stick!”. Said by the one who pushes him to the lake. Huh. . .

He didn’t respond to the one who called out to him, but instead, he grabs his stick that’s been submerged in the water and walks away.

Water's HP: 9999999

It’s impossible to defeat water.

He concluded that there’s no possible way to defeat the water.

“Hey! Where are you going!” He really doesn’t know when to stop!

Feeling that he is about to get pushed again, he turns around and swings his stick with both of his hands. *swings* The kid who pushes him got hit in the arms and was thrown away by the hit of the stick, leaving him lying sideways on the ground.

“AHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!”

He then looks at the kid he swings at. The kid was shouting and crying while holding his right shoulder. He approaches the kid, and once again swings his stick at him. *swings* And again. *swings* And again. *swings*

“AHHH!!! STOP!!!”

And again. *swings* And again. *swings* And again. *swings* And again.

“S-stop. . . Stop. . .”

After hearing it he stops midway his stance, and holds his stick across his shoulder.

“Weak.” He’s weaker than I thought.

He looks at the kid on the ground one final time. . . The kid was crying while lying on the ground, covering his eyes and face with both of his arms. Without saying anything he walks away, leaving the kid behind.

Kid’s HP: 7

He concluded that seven hits are enough to make a kid cry.

“Saro don’t stay out too late.” “Okay, Ma.”

Saro grabs his stick and once again walks outside to find something he can hit.

“What should I hit today?”

Walking under the blazing hot sun, he wanders through the area. As he was walking, he saw a figure of two persons, a kid and a mother walking side by side. Since they were walking slowly, he tried walking past them.

“Why is that kid holding a stick like that?”

The mother wondered why Saro was holding a stick like he is about to fight someone.

“THAT’S HIM MA!!!”, The kid shouts. “Him?” “The one who hits me!” “What?!”

After hearing that the mother rushes to Saro. She then holds Saro's right arm and steals the stick away.

WHAT! “Hey, what are you going to do with this stick?” “Give it back!”

Saro once again tries to steal it but was not able to.

“Look at my son!”

The mother not receiving any kind of response from Saro became agitated and pointed at her son. Upon hearing it, Saro stops stealing the stick and instead looks at the kid. The kid is full of bruises in his arms.

So that’s what happens when you get hit multiple times.

“Say sorry to him.” Why should I apologize? “. . .” “I said say sorry to him!” Arghhh this mother is so noisy!

Bad day for me, I hate this.
